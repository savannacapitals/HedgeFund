The Evolution of Capital
1760–1870
Industrial Capital
Steam, steel, and scale. Capital concentrates around physical production and extractive resource systems.
1944–1990
Global Capital Markets
Bretton Woods, petrodollars, and the rise of transnational financial institutions. Money becomes a geopolitical instrument.
2000–2015
Digital Finance
Electronic trading, algorithmic systems, and mobile money begin to decouple finance from physical infrastructure.
2015–2021
Blockchain Infrastructure
Distributed ledgers, smart contracts, and programmable value systems challenge the architecture of trust.
2021–Present
Artificial Intelligence
Machine intelligence enters every layer of financial infrastructure — from credit underwriting to macroeconomic modeling.
Emerging Now
Financial Intelligence Networks
The convergence of AI, blockchain, and real-time data creates sovereign-grade intelligence infrastructure.
