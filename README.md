Welcome to the central repository for **Savanna Capital**. This architecture operates at the intersection of a quantitative research firm and a financial technology infrastructure lab, engineered to track multi-asset distributions, model risk telemetry, and process macro yield metrics across Sub-Saharan capital markets.

This repository houses our public-facing terminal frontend and our dynamic, cloud-synchronized **Research Intelligence Hub**.

---

## 🦅 System Architecture Overview

The Savanna ecosystem is built to scale financial intelligence through automated data pipelines and decentralization primitives:

* **The Visual Terminal:** A Bloomberg-inspired, high-contrast, dark-mode terminal frontend optimized for high-velocity alpha discovery and structural macro visualization.
* **The Research Core Matrix:** A dynamic engine powered by a JavaScript/HTML framework that reads asynchronously from a live Supabase backend instance to serve real-time macroeconomic research and sovereign quantitative briefs.
* **Open-Source Sandbox:** A digital environment designed to intercept and train high-tier mathematical, engineering, and financial talent through real-world asset (RWA) tokenization models and stablecoin liquidity workflows.
